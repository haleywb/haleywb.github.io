     Haley, a native of Colorado, will be receiving her BA in Studio Art and minor in Technology, Arts, and Media in May of 2016. Afterwards, she hopes to become a full-time, free-lance commissioned artist.
     In her practice, she has attempted to stay both versatile and open-minded; she has experience with everything from the Adobe Suite to guitar building. Haley specializes in graphic design, including (but not limited to) logo and icon design, typography, and advertisement through fliers or posters.
     Despite her digital preference, her roots are in traditional drawing and painting, which translate into tattoo design, greeting cards, large scale murals, jewelry-making, woodworking, and more. Because of her wide range of skills, and her passion for the arts, she will remain open-minded to all requests and will always prioritize
the client.
